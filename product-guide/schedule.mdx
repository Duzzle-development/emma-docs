# Schedule

emma's scheduling feature allows you to automate tasks and set up recurring workflows with ease.

## Overview

With emma's Schedule feature, you can:

- **Automate recurring tasks** - Set up tasks to run automatically at specified intervals
- **Time-based triggers** - Execute actions based on specific dates and times
- **Smart scheduling** - Let emma optimize your schedule for maximum efficiency

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Schedule">
    Navigate to the Schedule section from your emma dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Schedule">
    Click "New Schedule" and configure your task settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Frequency">
    Choose how often you want the task to run (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate">
    Review your settings and activate the schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices
